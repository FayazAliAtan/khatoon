Our Story

Founded in 2010, Khatoon Designer has been redefining ethnic fashion with a perfect blend of traditional craftsmanship and contemporary designs. Our journey began with a small boutique in Karachi and has now grown into a beloved brand across Pakistan.

Each piece in our collection is meticulously designed and handcrafted by skilled artisans, ensuring exceptional quality and attention to detail. We take pride in celebrating Pakistan's rich textile heritage while innovating for the modern woman.
